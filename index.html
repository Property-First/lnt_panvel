Overview

L&T Panvel

L&T Panvel is an upcoming gated community in the thriving landscape of Panvel near the Navi Mumbai International Airport. The 2 BHK, 3 BHK, and 4 BHK apartments present enchanting views of the forest and hills. The thoughtfully designed apartments with grand decks allow uninterrupted ventilation and natural light. Everything in your residence exhibits finesse and class, from smooth finishes and quality floorings to high-end sanitary fittings. This sprawling community features abundant open spaces and an array of amenities, including a multipurpose sports court, indoor games zone, kids’ play area, an amphitheater, jogging track, swimming pool, etc.

L&T Panvel is in a gated community that is equipped with renowned social amenities such as Amity University, L&T Data Centre, St. Wilfred’s High School, MNR International School, Dhirubhai Ambani Hospital, Gandhi Super Speciality Hospital, Dmart, Reliance Smart, Orion Mall, and more. The Old Mumbai-Pune Highway and Mumbai-Pune Expressway are nearby and offer seamless connectivity across the city. The upcoming Chowk railway station will further enhance the connectivity in the region. Atal Setu makes South Mumbai accessible via the JNPT Road. The upcoming Navi Mumbai International Airport is also within a 30-minute drive from L&T Panvel, making it so convenient for regular commuters. So, invest in L&T Panvel, a premium address that keeps you engulfed in a progressive location.

About L&T Realty

Larsen & Toubro (L&T), headquartered in Mumbai, is a global conglomerate excelling in technology, construction, manufacturing, and financial services. With over 80 years of experience, L&T offers innovative solutions across vital sectors such as hydrocarbon, infrastructure, power, process industries, and defense.

The company’s dedication to customer satisfaction and uncompromising quality has positioned it as a leader in every venture it undertakes, supported by its high standards of corporate governance.
